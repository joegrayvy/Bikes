I bought Swen new in April 2021 after I fell in love with the colour scheme, you should always select a bike based on the colour and the look of it, if you don't like the look you'll never be happy riding it.


The Colour is listed as Carbon Chameleon Silver Green, the paint has a thin layup and you can see the naked carbon in places with a green and silver shimmer that comes through, especially vivid in the sun.
